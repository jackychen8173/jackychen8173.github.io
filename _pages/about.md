I'm a **Master's student in Applied Computing at BCIT** with a background in Computer Science and Microbiology & Immunology from UBC.

I specialize in **full-stack development** and **machine learning**, currently working on:
- **TutorSpace**: A tutoring management platform built with Spring Boot, Next.js, and PostgreSQL
- **Plant tissue growth simulation**: Computational biology research using C++ and the Tissue Simulator
- **Machine learning projects**: Including malware detection with LightGBM (F1: 0.96, ROC-AUC: 0.99) and indoor visual place recognition with DINOv2
- **Data visualization**: Interactive dashboards and analytics platforms using React, Plotly, and Power BI

My experience spans **full-stack development** (React, TypeScript, Spring Boot), **data science and ML** (Python, PyTorch, scikit-learn, LightGBM), and **data analytics** (R, SQL, Power BI, data visualization). I've built production data dashboards including a BC COVID-19 public health dashboard and the Youth Data Explorer for GLOCAL Foundation.

I bring a unique perspective combining software engineering, data analytics, and scientific research, with hands-on experience in data pipeline development, statistical analysis, and creating actionable insights from complex datasets.

I'm actively seeking **full-time opportunities** in full-stack, backend, ML engineering, or data analytics roles in Vancouver or remote.

When I'm not coding, you can find me tutoring students in math and science, playing soccer, or exploring new hobbies and interests.
